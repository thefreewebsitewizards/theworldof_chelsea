🇮🇹 3 Days in Rome

La Dolce Vita in the Eternal City

13 May 2025

Colossal ruins, Renaissance masterpieces, and rooftop aperitivos—Rome is a living museum where every corner tells a story. This 3-day itinerary blends ancient history with modern indulgence, ensuring you experience the best of Rome.

📅 Day 1: Ancient Wonders & Rooftop Sips

Morning:

  • Colosseum Tour: Begin your Roman adventure with a guided tour of the Colosseum. Booking in advance is recommended to skip the lines
  • Roman Forum & Palatine Hill: Wander through the heart of ancient Rome, exploring the ruins of temples, basilicas, and the palaces of emperors
    Combine all three experiences here

Afternoon:

  • Lunch: Enjoy traditional Roman dishes at a nearby trattoria
  • Trevi Fountain: Make your way to the iconic Trevi Fountain. Don't forget to toss a coin over your left shoulder to ensure your return to Rome

Evening:

  • Dinner: Indulge in classic Roman cuisine at a local restaurant

🛵 Day 2: Vatican Vistas & Vespa Adventures

Morning:

  • Vatican Museums & Sistine Chapel: Explore the vast art collections, culminating in Michelangelo's masterpiece on the Sistine Chapel ceiling
  • St. Peter's Basilica: Ascend to the dome for a breathtaking view of the city
    Book a combination ticket here

Afternoon:

  • Lunch: Dine at a café in the Vatican vicinity
  • Pantheon: Visit this architectural marvel, a testament to Rome's ancient ingenuity
  • Gelato Break: Treat yourself to artisanal gelato from a renowned gelateria

Evening:

  • Vespa Tour with Photoshoot: Experience Rome like a local on a Vespa, capturing memorable photos at iconic spots
    Book here
  • Dinner: Conclude the day with a delightful meal at a traditional osteria

🌳 Day 3: Gardens, Galleries & Spanish Steps

Morning:

  • Villa Borghese Gardens: Stroll through these lush gardens and rent a boat on the picturesque lake
  • Borghese Gallery: Admire masterpieces by Bernini, Caravaggio, and Raphael

Afternoon:

  • Lunch: Picnic in the gardens or dine at a nearby café
  • Spanish Steps: Descend these famous steps and explore the upscale shopping district

Evening:

  • Dinner: Enjoy your final Roman meal and savour cocktails with an unparalleled view of the Colosseum at the Royal Art Café's rooftop terrace
  • Evening Stroll: Take a leisurely walk through Rome's illuminated streets, soaking in the city's nocturnal charm

🛵 Getting Around

Electric Scooters: A fun and efficient way to navigate the city
Public Transport: Utilise Rome's metro and bus systems for longer distances
Walking: Many attractions are within walking distance, allowing you to discover hidden gems along the way

🏨 Where to Stay: Mercure Roma Centro Colosseo

Situated just steps from the Colosseum, the Mercure Roma Centro Colosseo offers modern comfort with a touch of history. Its rooftop terrace boasts a seasonal pool and bar, providing panoramic views of the Colosseum—perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration.

🍝 Travel Tips

Cuisine: Don't miss out on local specialties like Cacio e Pepe, Carbonara, and Roman-style pizza
Dress Code: When visiting religious sites, ensure your shoulders and knees are covered
Reservations: Book major attractions and popular restaurants in advance to avoid disappointment

Final Thoughts

In just three days, you'll have immersed yourself in Rome's rich tapestry of history, art, and gastronomy. From the grandeur of the Colosseum to the serenity of Villa Borghese, every moment promises to be memorable. Buon viaggio!
